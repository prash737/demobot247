# Conversations History

This document outlines the considerations for storing and managing conversation history within our system. It's crucial for providing context, improving user experience, and ensuring compliance with data privacy regulations.

## Purpose

The purpose of retaining conversation history is to:

*   Provide a seamless user experience by allowing users to refer back to previous interactions.
*   Enable our organization to analyze user interactions and identify areas for improvement in our operational process.
*   Facilitate training and quality assurance for support staff.
*   Comply with data privacy regulations.

## Scope

This policy applies to all conversations between users and our system, including:

*   Chatbot interactions
*   Email exchanges
*   Phone calls (where recordings are retained)
*   Support tickets

*   **Data Minimization:** We will only retain the minimum amount of data necessary to achieve the stated purposes.

## Data Security

*   Conversation history will be stored securely using [Specify Security Measures, e.g., encryption, access controls].
*   Access to conversation history will be restricted to authorized personnel only.
*   Regular audits will be conducted to ensure data security.

## User Rights

*   Users have the right to access their conversation history.
*   Users have the right to request the deletion of their conversation history, subject to legal and regulatory requirements.
*   Users have the right to correct any inaccuracies in their conversation history.

## Compliance

This policy is designed to comply with all applicable data privacy regulations.
