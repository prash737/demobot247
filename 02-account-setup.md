# Bot247 User Manual - Account Setup and Login

## Creating Your Account

### Sign Up Process

1. Visit [bot247.live](https://bot247.live) and click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner
2. Enter your email address (this will be your username)
3. Create a secure password
4. Click "Sign Up" to create your account
5. You'll be redirected to the profile setup page

### Profile Setup

After signing up, you'll need to complete your profile:

1. Enter your name and contact information
2. Select your organization type (business, non-profit, government, etc.)
3. Upload a profile picture (optional)
4. Click "Save" to continue

## Logging In

### Standard Login

1. Visit [bot247.live](https://bot247.live) and click on the "Login" button
2. Enter your email address and password
3. Click "Login" to access your dashboard

### Password Recovery

If you forget your password:

1. Click on the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for a password reset link
4. Click the link and follow the instructions to create a new password

## Subscription Plans

Bot247 offers three subscription plans to meet different needs:

### Basic Plan (Free)
- Free for all non-profit organizations / 14-day free trial for other organizations
- Up to 500 chat sessions per month
- Basic AI chatbot
- Website crawl data
- Email support
- Basic analytics and chat history
- Bot247.live branding

### Pro Plan (₹6,000/month)
- Unlimited chat sessions
- Advanced AI chatbot
- Up to 20 data sources (including URL, PDF, TXT, CSV)
- Priority support
- Advanced analytics & reporting
- Custom co-branding

### Advanced Plan (₹10,000/month)
- WhatsApp Business API registration and integration
- Unlimited chat sessions
- Advanced AI chatbot
- Up to 20 data sources (including URL, PDF, TXT, CSV)
- Priority support
- Advanced analytics & reporting
- Dedicated account manager
- Custom branding

### Selecting a Plan

1. After creating your account, you'll be prompted to select a subscription plan
2. Review the features of each plan
3. Click on your preferred plan
4. For paid plans, you'll be directed to the payment page
5. Complete the payment process using the secure payment gateway
6. Once payment is confirmed, your plan will be activated

### Changing Your Plan

You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time:

1. Go to your Profile page
2. Click on the "Plans" tab
3. Select your new desired plan
4. Follow the prompts to complete the change

## Account Management

### Updating Profile Information

1. Click on your profile icon in the top right corner
2. Select "Profile" from the dropdown menu
3. Update your information as needed
4. Click "Save Changes"

### Security Settings

To change your password:

1. Go to your Profile page
2. Click on the "Security" tab
3. Enter your current password
4. Enter and confirm your new password
5. Click "Update Password"

### Deleting Your Account

If you wish to delete your account:

1. Go to your Profile page
2. Click on the "Account" tab
3. Scroll to the bottom and click "Delete Account"
4. Confirm your decision by entering your password
5. Click "Permanently Delete Account"

**Note**: Deleting your account will permanently remove all your data, including chatbots, conversations, and analytics. This action cannot be undone.
