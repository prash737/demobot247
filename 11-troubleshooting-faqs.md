## Troubleshooting FAQs

This document provides answers to frequently asked questions to help you troubleshoot common issues.

**General Questions**

*   **Q: Who can use this platform?**
    A: This platform is designed for use by organizations.

*   **Q: How do I contact support?**
    A: You can contact our support team through the "Contact Us" page or by emailing support@example.com. We strive to respond to all user inquiries within 24 hours.

*   **Q: Where can I find more information about the platform's features?**
    A: Detailed information about all platform features can be found in the user manual, accessible through the "Help" menu.

**Account Issues**

*   **Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?**
    A: You can reset your password by clicking the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Follow the instructions sent to your registered email address.

*   **Q: My account is locked. What should I do?**
    A: Accounts are automatically locked after multiple failed login attempts. Please contact support to unlock your account.

*   **Q: How do I update my profile information?**
    A: You can update your profile information by navigating to the "Profile" section in your account settings.

**Data and Privacy**

*   **Q: How is my data protected?**
    A: We take data security very seriously. We adhere to strict data protection regulations and employ industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

*   **Q: Can I export my data?**
    A: Yes, you can export your data in various formats through the "Export Data" option in your account settings.

*   **Q: How long is my data stored?**
    A: Your data is stored as long as your account is active. You can request data deletion upon account closure.

**Usage Questions**

*   **Q: How do I add new users to my organization?**
    A: You can add new users through the "User Management" section in the admin panel.

*   **Q: I'm having trouble uploading files. What could be the issue?**
    A: Please ensure that your files meet the size and format requirements specified in the documentation. If the problem persists, contact support.

*   **Q: How can I track user interaction trends on the platform?**
    A: The platform provides analytics dashboards that allow you to monitor user interaction trends and gain insights into platform usage.

**Global User Information**

*   **Q: Is the platform available in multiple languages?**
    A: Yes, the platform supports multiple languages. You can select your preferred language in your account settings.

*   **Q: Are there any resources available for global users?**
    A: We provide documentation and support in multiple languages to assist our global users.

**Pricing and Billing**

*   **Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
    A: We accept major credit cards and bank transfers.

*   **Q: Are there non-profit discounts available?**
    A: Yes, we offer non-profit discounts to eligible organizations. Please contact our sales team for more information.

*   **Q: How do I update my billing information?**
    A: You can update your billing information through the "Billing" section in your account settings.

**Organizations Specific Questions**

*   **Q: How do I manage user roles within my organization?**
    A: User roles can be managed through the "User Management" section in the admin panel.

*   **Q: Can I customize the platform's branding for my organization?**
    A: Yes, we offer customization options for organizations. Please contact our sales team to discuss your specific needs.

*   **Q: How do I integrate the platform with other systems used by my organization?**
    A: We provide APIs and integration tools to facilitate seamless integration with other systems. Please refer to the API documentation for more information.
