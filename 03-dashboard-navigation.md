# Bot247 User Manual - Dashboard and Navigation

## Dashboard Overview

After logging in, you'll be directed to your Bot247 dashboard, which serves as your central hub for managing all aspects of your chatbot. The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of your chatbot's performance and quick access to all platform features.

### Dashboard Elements

1. **Header Bar**: Contains your profile menu, notifications, and help resources
2. **Main Navigation**: Sidebar menu for accessing different sections of the platform
3. **Quick Stats**: Overview of key metrics like total conversations and user queries
4. **Recent Activity**: Latest interactions with your chatbot
5. **Usage Meter**: Shows your current plan usage and limits

## Navigation Structure

The Bot247 platform is organized into several key sections, each accessible from the main navigation sidebar:

### Dashboard
The home screen showing performance overview and key metrics.

### Chatbot Setup
- **Knowledge**: Manage your chatbot's knowledge base
- **Instructions**: Configure your chatbot's behavior and responses
- **Theme**: Customize your chatbot's appearance
- **Embed**: Get code to integrate your chatbot on your website

### Analytics
- **Overview**: High-level performance metrics
- **Conversations**: Detailed conversation logs
- **User Queries**: Analysis of common questions
- **Reports**: Generate and export detailed reports

### Account
- **Profile**: Manage your personal information
- **Subscription**: View and change your plan
- **Billing**: Access invoices and payment history
- **Team**: Invite and manage team members (Pro and Advanced plans)

## Key Navigation Features

### Responsive Design
The Bot247 dashboard is fully responsive and works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices, allowing you to manage your chatbot from anywhere.

### Keyboard Shortcuts
For power users, Bot247 offers keyboard shortcuts for common actions:
- `D`: Navigate to Dashboard
- `K`: Navigate to Knowledge Base
- `T`: Navigate to Theme Customization
- `A`: Navigate to Analytics
- `?`: Show all keyboard shortcuts

To use these shortcuts, press `Shift + ?` to view the complete list.

### Search Functionality
The global search bar at the top of the dashboard allows you to quickly find specific conversations, settings, or help topics:

1. Click on the search icon or press `Ctrl + /` (`Cmd + /` on Mac)
2. Type your search query
3. Select from the results to navigate directly to that item

### Notifications
The bell icon in the header displays important notifications about your chatbot:

- System updates
- Conversation alerts
- Usage limits
- Payment reminders

Click on a notification to view more details or take action.

## Customizing Your Dashboard

You can personalize your dashboard view to focus on the metrics that matter most to you:

1. Click the "Customize" button in the top right of the dashboard
2. Drag and drop widgets to rearrange them
3. Toggle visibility for specific metrics
4. Adjust the time period for displayed data
5. Click "Save Layout" to preserve your changes

## Mobile App Access

Bot247 also offers a mobile app for on-the-go management:

1. Download the Bot247 app from the App Store or Google Play
2. Log in with your existing credentials
3. Access a streamlined version of the dashboard optimized for mobile devices

**Note**: Some advanced customization features may be limited in the mobile app.
